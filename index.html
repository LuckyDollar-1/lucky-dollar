About Lucky Dollar

Our planet is home to over 8 billion people, yet a significant percentage live on the edge of poverty. The Lucky Dollar project creates a community where small contributions from many can provide real help through a fair, transparent monthly lottery.

By donating just $1, each participant enters a raffle where the pooled funds go to one lucky winner. We keep only 13% to support the project’s growth and sustainability, allowing more people to join and benefit.

This platform promotes global solidarity, hope, and practical assistance. Join us in making a difference — one dollar at a time.
