Lucky Dollar

There are over 8 billion people on Earth. Imagine if even a small percentage donated just $1 to help those in need. Lucky Dollar is a global social experiment that collects $1 donations and randomly gives the accumulated amount (minus 13% for promotion and operation) to one lucky donor each month.

Every donation is a seed of hope. Whether you win or not, you help someone get closer to a better life. This is about unity, compassion, and chance.

We've added a 13% allocation for spreading the idea and maintaining the project. This allows more people to join in and creates a bigger global impact. It's not a fee — it's an investment into our collective momentum.
