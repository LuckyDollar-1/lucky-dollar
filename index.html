Lucky Dollar

There are nearly 8 billion people on Earth, yet a significant portion live on the edge of poverty.

Lucky Dollar is a community-driven project aiming to help those in need by pooling small donations.

Each participant donates $1 monthly to a shared prize pool. At the end of each month, one lucky donor receives the entire prize (minus a 13% fee used to promote and maintain the project).

With just one dollar, you can join this global movement to bring hope and change lives.
